## Goal

Implement FedProx training with µ grid `{0.0, 0.001, 0.01, 0.1, 1.0}`. Verify µ=0.0 equivalence with FedAvg. Produce FedProx checkpoints, scores, and metrics for stress-test evaluation.

## Why This Exists

FedProx is a locked aggregation-side stress test. It tests whether the B1→B2 CV(FPR) gain survives a heterogeneity-aware aggregation variant.

## Scope

- Implement FedProx training (proximal term in local loss)
- µ grid from config: `{0.0, 0.001, 0.01, 0.1, 1.0}`
- E (local epochs) equals FedAvg E
- µ=0.0 sanity check: must match FedAvg within tolerance
- Produce checkpoints and scores per (µ, seed)
- Convergence status reporting

## Out of Scope

- Adding new µ values after results
- Tuning FedProx to rescue results
- Making FedProx a core baseline
- Running on Regime D/B-b (conditional on those tickets)
