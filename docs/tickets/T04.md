## Goal

Combine GA-02 and GA-03 outputs to emit a final `VERIFIED_REUSE_SAFE` or `REUSE_BLOCKED_RERUN_REQUIRED` verdict per cell. Produce a cell verdict table that gates all subsequent Group GB analyses.

## Why This Exists

Group GB stored-score analyses are only allowed on cells with `VERIFIED_REUSE_SAFE`. This ticket produces the formal gating artifact.

## Scope

- Consume T02 verification reports and T03 reproduction results
- Apply the conjunction: manifest valid AND metrics reproduce → `VERIFIED_REUSE_SAFE`
- Produce a cell verdict table (CSV/JSON) listing every cell and its verdict
- Any cell failing either check gets `REUSE_BLOCKED_RERUN_REQUIRED`

## Out of Scope

- Deciding whether to rerun blocked cells (that is an experiment decision)
- Implementing rerun logic
