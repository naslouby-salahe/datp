## Goal

Train seeds 5–9 for Regime A (and optionally Regime B-a and Regime C) to produce 10-seed results for stronger journal claims. Compute 10-seed BCa CI.

## Why This Exists

Current repository has seeds 0–4 only (`SEEDS_5_ONLY_EXTENSION_REQUIRED`). For a journal-strength confirmatory claim, 10 seeds are needed. The 10-seed BCa CI is the primary confirmatory statistic.

## Scope

- Train shared FL encoder for seeds 5–9 on Regime A
- Score all clients for seeds 5–9
- Compute B1/B2/B3/B4 thresholds and metrics for seeds 5–9
- Compute 10-seed BCa CI on per-seed Δ_s = CV(FPR)[B1,s] − CV(FPR)[B2,s]
- Report x/10 seeds with positive Δ_s
- Optionally extend Regime B-a and C if compute allows

## Out of Scope

- Changing the training protocol for seeds 5–9 (must be identical to 0–4)
- Superseding 5-seed results with anything other than 10-seed results
- Tuning after seeing 10-seed results
