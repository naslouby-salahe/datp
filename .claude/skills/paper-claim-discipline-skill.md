# paper-claim-discipline-skill

## Purpose

Keep paper claims narrow, honest, and evidence-bound.

## Claim Categories

1. Direct evidence
2. Supportive evidence
3. Exploratory evidence
4. Reference comparison
5. Limitation
6. Future work

## Forbidden Unsupported Claims

Do not claim:

1. General anomaly detection superiority
2. Poisoning robustness
3. Backdoor robustness
4. Evasion robustness
5. Formal privacy guarantee
6. Secure aggregation guarantee
7. Real hardware deployment readiness
8. Concept drift handling
9. Zero-day malware detection
10. New aggregation contribution

## Required Output

1. Claim reviewed
2. Evidence category
3. Supported wording
4. Unsafe wording
5. Replacement wording
